FAQs

Typing speed is typically measured in words per minute (WPM), which is calculated by dividing the number of words typed by the time taken in minutes.

Typing speed is influenced by factors such as familiarity with the keyboard layout, muscle memory, practice, accuracy, and the complexity of the text being typed.

Typing speed can be tested online using various typing test websites that provide timed typing challenges, calculate words per minute (WPM), and often provide feedback on accuracy and errors.

Methods to improve typing speed include practicing regularly, using typing software or games, learning proper finger placement, and focusing on accuracy before speed.

There is no point in typing fast if you type a bundle of wrong words. You can try being fast until you are more than 95% accurate. Anything less than that is not acceptable. The end goal should always be typing with speed but maintaining a near to perfection or 100% accuracy.
